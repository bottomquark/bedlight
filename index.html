What this is about

Waking up and walking out of bedroom having the choice of either turning on the main lights and being blinded instantly or using your shinbone to find furniture in the dark room? Both options are not that much fun.

A solution would be: using a motion sensor to turn on red light which lets you see something without being blinded.
